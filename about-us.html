ABOUT US

Hotel Grandeur

Hotel Grandeur is, conveniently located in the heart of the city. This city hotel is very close to the sakinaka metro station and just a 15 min minute’s drive from the airport. The hotel is centrally located and hence very convenient for easy access to all the local activities and attractions. It is near many famous malls, restaurants, cafes, bars, and nightclubs as well as the famous shopping center.

Already listed in OTA, goibibo, MMT, and Agoda, it is newly revamped with modern styling offering an excellent standard of accommodation in a quiet, calm, and fashionable environment again at affordable prices. In addition to the trendy look of the space, lobby, and rooms; great emphasis has been given to quality, comfort, and hygiene of the guest, as well as a sense of luxury at peak. The rooms at Hotel Grandeur are equipped with all contemporary amenities and modern facilities. The hotel also offers free Wi-Fi in all rooms and public areas, free parking facilities close to the hotel, and a 24*7 dedicated support staff. Whenever you are on a holiday or on a business trip, to start your day, the hotel offers an endless selection of hot & cold breakfast buffets, prepared under the strictest standards of hygiene.
